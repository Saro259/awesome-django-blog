About Me


John Solly

Linkedin | Github Youtube Channel

Know anyone with a medical grade EEG to monitor their sleep? That's the kind of stuff I do! My goal in life is to optimize my faith, family, friends, and career so I am 1% better every day in every way. I have my B.A. in GIS and Remote sensing from UC Santa Barbara and a M.S. in Geoinformatics and Geospatial Intelligence (that's a mouthful!) from George Mason University.

My master's thesis on Team Spatial Cognition was funded by the Army Research Institute. Team Spatial Cognition is the idea that groups of people can communally develop shared maps in their heads. If you want to learn more, check out my thesis!

I created this blog as a way to strengthen my web development skills and share my thoughts with the world. Stay tuned for updates!
