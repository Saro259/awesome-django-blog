My Bio

I strive to be the most well-rounded full-stack developer in the Geo community. This journey began at UC Santa Barbara, where I studied GIS and remote sensing. I interned on several research teams, interacting with GIS thought leaders such as Michael Goodchild, Waldo Tobler, Krzysztof Janowicz, and Dan Montello.

After graduating with honors, I traveled to the opposite coast to research Team Spatial Cognition at George Mason University, a project funded partly by the Army Research Institute. I lectured undergraduate GIS courses as a TA, acted as president of the ASPRS Potomac Region Chapter, and presented my research at AAG's annual conference in San Francisco.

After completing an M.S. in Geoinformatics and Geospatial Intelligence, I started working at a leading GIS company, Esri. I was fortunate to be in the Washington D.C. regional office, where I interacted with members of Congress and many government organizations, including the NGA, NASA, and EPA. I then spent four years as a product engineer at Esri HQ in Redlands, CA, where I helped launch ArcGIS Dashboards out of beta to become the most viewed GIS web application in the world.

My next role exposed me to GIS' cousin, Business Intelligence. At YellowfinBI, I advised developers on Yellowfin's integration patterns using SOAP, REST, and JS APIs. After Idera Inc. acquired Yellowfin in February 2022, I took a year off work to dive deep into geospatial web development with Python and TypeScript. I built web experiences with Django, Node, and React that integrate web mapping libraries, tools, and applications. I combine my experience with a love of computer science, and a passion for writing secure, tested, and performant geospatial applications.

Do you have complex geo-problems that need a geospatial expert to solve? Drop me a message, and let's explore our fit together!
