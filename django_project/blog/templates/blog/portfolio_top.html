Hello World, I'm John!

My Bio

As a geospatial developer, I combine GIS domain knowledge and full stack web development to create beautiful (and fast) mapping applications.

I studied GIS under thought leaders (Michael Goodchild, Waldo Tobler, Krzysztof Janowicz, and Dan Montello) while completing a B.A. in Geography at UC Santa Barbara and an M.S. in Geoinformatics and Geospatial Intelligence at George Mason University.

I've actioned this GIS knowledge in several academic capacities, including as a geospatial researcher funded by the Army Research Institute, a TA lecturing undergraduate courses, and the ASPRS Potomac Region president.

Upon graduation, I joined Esri to launch ArcGIS Dashboards out of beta, becoming one of the world's most viewed geospatial web applications. I also worked at a business intelligence startup, Yellowfin, where I fused the world of GIS and BI as a technical consultant, advising developers on integration best practices.

These skills and experiences have made me a versatile GIS professional who can solve a variety spatial problems. If you have complex geospatial challenges, Drop me a message, and let's explore our fit together!
